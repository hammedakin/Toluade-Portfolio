REVEAL

Women have generally been known as homemakers for years. Many of our mothers were left at home to take care of the home and the kids while the men went out to make the money to fend for the family. There is usually this myth that the women have little or nothing to do at home as their only job was to just sit and do chores. This project seek to show the stress women go through to make our home a comfortable place to stay while they try to make little cash by the side using Mummy Mariam as a case study.
