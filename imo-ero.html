IMO-ERO

Years ago, Technology was introduced into our lives and it was a huge upgrade from landlines and big computers whose major purpose was to receive calls and process data. Technology brought us closer to the world and the world closer to us. However we have become dependent on them,Exacerbating attention deficit disorders are on the increase.Lonliness and depression are increasingly popular and diminishing ability to think creatively and concentrate. Many admit to be safer with their phones and other gadgets than without it, we are prisoners to the gadgets. Tolu Adekuoroye conceived this series of images to address the reality of this addiction and the damage it brings to our world today.
