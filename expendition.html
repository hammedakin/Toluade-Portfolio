EXPENDITION

“Expedition” an ongoing project that seek to express the difficulty of my pregnancy journey especially the first few months and it’s effects on my life, Art and family. This project seek to draw attention to the struggle women have, how it impact their surroundings and mental health as they journey through to the final gratification of pregnancy.
